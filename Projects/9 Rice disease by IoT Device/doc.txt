Jun 2023 - Jul 2023Jun 2023 - Jul 2023
This project aims to detect rice diseases using the ESP32 Cam, perform disease classification using
YOLOv5 in Python, display the results on a 16x2 LCD display, send SMS notifications to the farmer,
and provide real-time notifications through a mobile app. MQTT protocol is used for IoT data transfer.
